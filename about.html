Cerys and Megan are two sisters passionate about healthy living and nutritious food. In 2015, they opened up a juice bar called ZestHouse to provide a healthy and convenient option for those wanting to nourish their bodies with natural, wholesome ingredients. Over time, ZestHouse became a fixture in the community, and Cerys and Megan continue to help people live their best, healthiest lives, one sip of juice at a time.

To ensure our juice bar can be enjoyed by all, our toilets were made fully accessible to those with physical disabilities. We also have baby changing facilities on site.
We have a designated quiet working area, equipped with plug sockets and usb charging ports, whether you're a remote worker, a student, or simply want to enjoy a good book over a glass of juice.
Got an occasion coming up? ZestHouse can be rented out for private events, during which you are welcome to bring your own food, such as a birthday cake!.
